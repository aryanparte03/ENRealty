Privacy policy

At EN Realty, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. When you connect with us—by contacting, using our services, or browsing our website—we may collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, and property preferences. This information enables us to provide personalized real estate services, relevant communication, and a seamless experience with EN Realty.

Your trust is a top priority. We do not sell, lease, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Disclosure may only occur when necessary for completing real estate transactions, legal compliance, or responding to official requests from authorities.

To ensure your data security, EN Realty uses industry-standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. We continuously review and update our privacy protocols to maintain the highest possible data protection standards.

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. You can also opt out of marketing communications by reaching out to us. If we make revisions to this Privacy Policy, we will display the new version on our website with the appropriate effective date. Please check periodically to remain informed about how EN Realty protects your information.

If you have questions, requests, or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or data handling, contact us at [your email address]. We’re here to provide answers and support your privacy rights.
